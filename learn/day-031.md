## Performance and stability notes
- Data quality often dominates over marginal optimizer tweaks in chat quality outcomes.
- RL loops can be unstable; monitor for collapse, reward hacking, or regressions.
- Always compare checkpoints with fixed prompt sets and decoding settings.

## Common failure modes
- Assuming SFT gains transfer to all evaluation regimes.
- Interpreting reward improvements as capability improvements without external eval.
- Failing to lock prompt/eval settings when comparing checkpoints.
